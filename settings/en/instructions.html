In order for the verification to take place, you must allow the plugin to access the downloaded files through your browser. To do so, please follow these instructions:

Finish install

  1. In th top right, click on « more » (three verticla dots) .

  2. Go to « More tools » then, « Extensions ».

  3. click on details for the extension named « Download and Verify Checksum ».

  4. Allow access to file URLs.
